# Med-Tracker ET

> Your digital bridge to better medication management

## 📋 About the App

Med-Tracker ET is an Android app that helps Ethiopian patients locate nearby pharmacies with their medications in stock, track their prescription schedules, and avoid missed or incorrect doses. It bridges the gap between patients and their medications in a healthcare system strained by frequent drug stockouts and irrational medication use.

## ✅ Core MVP Features

- **Medication Reminder & Scheduler** — Daily reminders with correct dosage instructions
- **Pharmacy Stock Locator** — Find nearby pharmacies with your prescription in stock
- **Prescription Tracker** — Log active prescriptions, dosage history, and refill dates
- **Medication Information Guide** — Basic info on common medications and side effects
